It's easy and Very Rewarding

Be part of a voice for change

Dignity/USA gives the hope and confidence to build a better world. We affirm and support our members. As the oldest and largest independent group of its kind, Dignity/USA can speak up for you, for changes needed if our church is to thrive in the 21st Century. Your membership in Dignity is your way of saying that you support what Dignity is doing in your behalf. Join today. Dignity does not and never has made membership data available to anyone.

Become a member and receive:

The Journal -- Illustrated National quarterly magazine, covering developments in the church and in Dignity. You'll also find members' viewpoints, practical justice and peace ideas, a public voice against injustice in the church, and reports from people like you on how Christians are responding positively to the challenges of church and society.

Dateline -- Monthly National newsletter with items of interest to you. Short articles give current news, the good and the bad, in the ongoing struggle to realize the Vatican II vision of the church as the people of God.

Local Chapter Newsletter -- If you wish to affiliate with a chapter (of your choice) you can also receive that chapter's newsletter, with local items of interest, thought provoking articles, and a calendar of events.
